# Abstracts

You will need to prepare 3 abstracts for you thesis

1. The defense
2. The teaser page for the dissertation
3. The dissertation itself

## 1. The defense

A fairly short and punch abstract to get people interested in coming to your thesis defense (talk). Stat dept emails these out a month in advance. Check with your own dept admin if not in stats. If in stats, email Susie the abstract you need (sooner that better), following the exact format of previous stat oral defense emails (same font, i.e. bold title, etc. etc). You will need to provide keywords, location, time etc. Look at recent examples from stat oral defenses.

## 2. Teaser for dissertation

This will show up on the webpage landing for your dissertation. It is mean to be a shorter version of 3. Probably try to keep it under 1920 characters (makes arxiv submission easier).

## 3. In dissertation

This is page 4 of your dissertation (page 1 is title page; page 2 is copyright page; page 3 is signature page. Pages 2-3 and autogenerated.)

You can be lengthier here, but probably keep it to a page.
